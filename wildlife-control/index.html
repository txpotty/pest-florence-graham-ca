Bat Exclusion and Control

Bats in your attic may not seem like a major issue initially, but they can create significant problems for property owners in Florence-Graham California. Signs of a bat presence include visible bats flying around dusk or dawn, staining or droppings on exterior walls below entry points, and chirping or scratching sounds from the attic or walls.
